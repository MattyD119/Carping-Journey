French Beauties

It has now been 5 years since I have been fishing and I one day found myself on the bank at Hawkhurst Fish Farm. I stare out into the lake with a renewed sense of excitment but also a bit of trepedation because I felt that I had forgotten how to catch carp. I wasn't particularly succesful that session either so when I next visited the tackle shop only to discover that Korda were giving away a free DVD with 4 hours of content I was excited to say the least. These were the days before YouTube and watching content like this for the first time was amazing and within a few days I had watched the whole thing and rewatched the sections on areas that helped in the areas that I felt a little rusty in like rig making.

Now I felt up to date it was time to get back to the lake and instantly I felt like a renewed angler and this showed in my results. I started to have success and my hooked to landed ratio was excellent.
